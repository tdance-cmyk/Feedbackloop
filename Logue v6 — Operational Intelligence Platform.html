The Discard Rule — Your Words Cannot Be Traced Back to You
When you submit, your text is sent to the AI and then immediately deleted — it is never written to the database. The system stores only: department, energy level, productivity score, topic tags, and time lost. These are saved with no link to your name or user ID. This is enforced at the database level, not just policy.
Technically Anonymous — Not Just a Policy Promise
A database row linking an AI insight to a specific user ID does not exist. Row-Level Security in the database makes it architecturally impossible for managers to retrieve your entry, even if they wanted to.
